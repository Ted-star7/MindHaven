What is Mental Health?

Mental health refers to a person's emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It influences how individuals think, feel, and behave in their daily lives. Good mental health helps people cope with stress, build relationships, work productively, and make meaningful contributions to their communities.

Mental health is not merely the absence of mental illness; it encompasses the presence of positive characteristics such as resilience, balance, and the ability to enjoy life. Factors affecting mental health include biological influences (e.g., genetics, brain chemistry), life experiences (e.g., trauma, abuse), and family history of mental health conditions.

Mental Health Topics

Early Warning Signs & Symptoms

  • Persistent sadness or low mood.
  • Excessive worry or fear.
  • Withdrawal from friends, family, and activities.
  • Changes in sleep or appetite.
  • Difficulty concentrating or making decisions.
  • Irritability or frequent mood swings.
  • Physical symptoms like headaches or stomachaches without a clear cause.

Mental Health Conditions

  • Depression: Characterized by prolonged sadness, lack of energy, and disinterest in activities.
  • Anxiety Disorders: Involves excessive worry, fear, or panic attacks.
  • Bipolar Disorder: Extreme mood swings ranging from mania (high energy) to depression.
  • Schizophrenia: Affects perception and thinking, leading to delusions or hallucinations.
  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD): Triggered by traumatic events, causing flashbacks, nightmares, or severe anxiety.

Tips for Living Well with a Mental Health Condition

Stay Active

Regular physical activity can improve mood and reduce symptoms.

Build a Support Network

Maintain connections with friends, family, or support groups.

Adopt Healthy Habits

Eat a balanced diet, sleep well, and avoid substance abuse.

Practice Mindfulness

Techniques like meditation and yoga can reduce stress.

Seek Professional Help

Therapy, counseling, and medication (if prescribed) can manage symptoms effectively.
